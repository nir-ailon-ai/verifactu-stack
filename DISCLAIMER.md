## Before going live

At minimum, before flipping any invoice submission to `--env=produccion`, you
should:

1. **Read the underlying regulation directly** — Real Decreto 1007/2023 and
   Orden HAC/1177/2024 are the primary sources.
2. **Consult a qualified gestor or fiscal advisor** familiar with Verifactu.
3. **Audit the code** and dependencies yourself. Do not treat this project as
   a black box.
4. **Exercise the full flow in preproducción** for the invoice types you
   actually issue, until you have high confidence in the output.
5. **Have a backup and rollback plan** for the local `verifactu_submissions`
   table and the FS database.
6. **Understand that fiscal actions are irreversible**. AEAT does not allow
   deletion of registered records; corrections happen exclusively through
   facturas rectificativas.
